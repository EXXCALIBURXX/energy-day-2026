About Energy Day

Energy Day is the flagship annual event of the Department of Energy Science and Engineering at IIT Bombay. It serves as a platform to foster innovation, collaboration, and knowledge exchange in the field of energy.

Over two days, students, academics, industry professionals, and researchers converge to explore cutting-edge technologies, participate in competitions, and network with leading figures in the energy sector.
