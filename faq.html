Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Energy Day 2026

Have Questions? We Have Answers

What is Energy Day?
+
Energy Day is the flagship annual event of the Department of Energy Science and Engineering at IIT Bombay. It brings together students, academics, industry professionals, and researchers to foster innovation, collaboration, and knowledge exchange in the energy sector.
Is registration free?
+
Yes, registration is free for all students and academic attendees. Corporate participants may have different registration policies—please check the registration page for details.
When and where is Energy Day 2026?
+
Energy Day 2026 will be held on April 5-6, 2026 at IIT Bombay Research Park, Powai, Mumbai. Check the Events tab for the complete schedule.
Who can attend?
+
Energy Day welcomes students from all disciplines, faculty members, industry professionals, researchers, and anyone interested in the energy sector. No prior experience is necessary.
Can I participate in competitions?
+
Absolutely! We have hackathons, case challenges, and quiz competitions. Visit the Competitions tab to learn about each event, requirements, and how to register your team.
Are accommodations provided?
+
Accommodations on the IIT Bombay campus may be available on a limited basis. Contact energyday@iitb.ac.in for specific inquiries regarding stay arrangements.
How can I network with industry?
+
Energy Day features dedicated networking sessions and a career fair where you can directly interact with representatives from leading energy companies, discuss internships, and explore career opportunities.
Can I present my research?
+
Yes! We have dedicated Student Presentation sessions. Submit your abstract through the registration portal or contact the organizing committee for more information on the submission process.
