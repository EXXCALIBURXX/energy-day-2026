Highlights 2025

A timeline of innovation, collaboration, and breakthroughs from our last edition.

June 10, 2025

Opening Address

The programme opened with a rapid-fire set of lightning talks showcasing applied research and community projects, setting the stage for a weekend of innovation.

June 10, 2025 - 2:00 PM

Deep-Dive Workshops

Hands-on deep-dive sessions focusing on energy analytics, storage modelling, and distributed sensing. Over 200 participants engaged in real-time data analysis.

June 11, 2025

Student Projects Showcase

Student teams presented prototypes for low-cost monitoring and demand-response pilot schemes. The winning team demonstrated a novel EV charging optimization algorithm.

June 11, 2025 - 5:00 PM

Panel Discussion: Green Hydrogen

Industry experts debated the scalability of Green Hydrogen in the Indian context, providing insights into future job markets and research opportunities.

June 12, 2025

Closing & Awards Night

A community evening celebrating projects and announcing follow-up pilot grants. Networking dinner allowed students to connect with top recruiters.
